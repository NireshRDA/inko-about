Studio

The Studio is a space for movement, exercise, meditation and learning. It is home to the Taekwondo Academy, offering regular classes led by a Kukkiwon-trained master and to the Yoga School, led by an Ashtanga-yoga specialist teacher. This is also the space for workshops led by specialist facilitators and practitioners across the performing and visual arts. Move, breathe, learn, share and rejuvenate…

Dokebi - The Café

The Café serves home-cooked, organic fresh food blending traditional Korean cuisine with contemporary variations. Whether aromatic tea blends, traditional patbingsu, juices or light meals, the Café epitomizes slow food, in an cosy, energized space. Eat, drink, taste, relax and re-energise…
