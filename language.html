Language

What does it take to bridge the cultural divide and what role does language learning play in allowing access and appreciation of cultures other than one's own? Whether it is a fascination for learning a foreign language; a purposeful step towards enhancing employment opportunities with Korean companies in India or abroad or to appreciate the differing business and management cultures of Indian and Korean corporates, our programmes, led by experienced faculty from Korea are practical, customised and purpose-driven.

Our Korean Language programme includes classes at InKo Centre, at Korean companies in Chennai and at select Universities in Chennai.
