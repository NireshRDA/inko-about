Performing Arts

Performing Arts

We aim to present high quality performances across the following art forms:

Theatre Music Dance Film

The focus is on examining the manner in which traditional techniques across these art forms are recreated to present exciting new forms that represent contemporary Korea.
While presenting interesting and innovative examples of Korean contemporary performing arts, we also actively look out for opportunities for Indo-Korean co-productions that meaningfully examine, through research and development the creation of new, vibrant Indo-Korean collaborative productions. All co-productions are developed in association with relevant partners in Korea and India.
