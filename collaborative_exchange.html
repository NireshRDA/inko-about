Collaborative Exchange

THEATRE

Heungbu-Nolbu: the first Indo-Korean Children’s Theatre production co-commissioned by InKo Centre and Theatre Seoul.

Hamlet_Avataar: an Indo-Korean theatre production, directed by Hyoung-taek Limb, Artistic Director of the Seoul Factory for the Performing Arts, South Korea. The production was developed by the Seoul Factory for the Performing Arts and InKo Centre with support from Arts Council Korea (ARKO).

Fish, Tree, Moon, directed by Yosup Bae, Artistic Director of Performance Group Tuida with Indian actors and musicians and an Indo-Korean crew. This production was co-produced by InKo Centre and Ranga Shankara.

MUSIC

Same Same But Different (SSBD) a collaboration that brought together Noreum Machi, from Korea, contemporary Indian dancer, Astad Deboo and three Indian musicians, percussionist, BC Manjunath, singer and flautist, Varijashree Venugopal and percussionist, composer and music director, Praveen D. Rao. This co-production was supported by The Ministry of Culture Sports and Tourism, Republic of Korea, Arts Council Korea, Carrot Global and Korea Mecenat Association.
