WaterBodies: an annual Indo-Korean Arts Residency project focusing on the theme of 'water' was held at Spaces, Chennai with 5 Indian and Korean artists and 1 curator from India and Korea. Their work was presented at the exhibition thereafter. Several multi-disciplinary talks and film screenings on the theme of water complemented the two-week long exhibition.

The first of the developmental artistic residencies for Beyond Binary-an Indo-Korean theatre collaboration directed by Yosup Bae, Artistic Director of Performance Group Tuida, commenced in Korea. The production is co-produced by AsiaNow Productions and InKo Centre and includes an Indian and Korean cast and crew.

01-Mar-2014

A preview of Beyond Binary - an Indo-Korean Theatre collaboration was held at Tagore Hall, Kalakshetra, Chennai with a select group of theatre practitioners and cultural aficionadas invited to critique the performance and provide feedback.

13-Mar-2014

InKo Centre, in association with the Korea Foundation and with support from the Consulate General of the Republic of Korea in Chennai, presented - Four Walls by Sincha Hong, one of the foremost avant-garde dancers from Korea. It was a meditative performance that paid tribute to the legendary composer and musician, John Cage. The performance was presented at the Sir Mutha Venkatasubba Rao Concert Hall, Chennai.
