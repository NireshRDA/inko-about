Exhibition

Best viewed on full-screen mode. After entering the exhibition, please click on the Menu on the right of the screen to open to full screen, to start your tour and to access relevant details about the exhibition.

STEPS TO VIEW THE EXHIBITION

Step 1:

• Click Enter Exhibition.
• Do not click Start Guided Tour.

Step 2:

• Go to Menu and click Full Screen for the best view.

Step 3:

• Click on the text highlighted in the circle.

Step 4:

• Navigate with ‘Next’ and ‘Previous’ buttons to view the exhibition.


Note: This exhibition provides multiple views of the objects on display.
